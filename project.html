Projects

This will be the section where the approved projects will be listed, so based on the interest we can apply to the projects to be part of it. The below projects are just the sample ones.

Project 1: Mars Exploration

Category: Space Exploration

Join our team in exploring the Red Planet, Mars! We're looking for passionate individuals with expertise in robotics, geology, and data analysis to analyze Martian soil samples and plan rover missions. Help us uncover the mysteries of Mars.

Skills Required: Robotics, Geology, Data Analysis

Time Commitment: 10-15 hours/week

Privacy: Public

Project Creator: Sanil Dulal


Project 2: Cosmic Observation

Category: Astronomy

Embark on a journey to observe and document cosmic phenomena! Our project focuses on capturing celestial events, and we're seeking astronomy enthusiasts with experience in telescopes, astrophysics, and data visualization. Let's explore the universe together.

Skills Required: Telescopes, Astrophysics, Data Visualization

Time Commitment: 5-10 hours/week

Privacy: Private


Project 3: Climate Change Analysis

Category: Earth Science

Contribute to understanding and addressing climate change! Our research team is looking for data scientists, meteorologists, and environmental scientists to analyze climate data, predict trends, and propose solutions. Together, we can make a difference.

Skills Required: Data Science, Meteorology, Environmental Science

Time Commitment: 15-20 hours/week

Privacy: Public


Project 4: Lunar Geology Study

Category: Space Exploration

Explore the lunar surface with us! We're assembling a team of geologists, astronauts, and researchers to analyze lunar rock samples. Help us unlock the secrets of the Moon's geological history and its potential for future missions.

Skills Required: Geology, Astronomy, Field Research

Time Commitment: 10-15 hours/week

Privacy: Public


Project 5: Exoplanet Discovery

Category: Astronomy

Join our mission to discover new exoplanets! We're searching for astronomers, data analysts, and telescope operators to identify and characterize exoplanetary systems. Help us expand our understanding of distant worlds beyond our solar system.

Skills Required: Astronomy, Data Analysis, Telescope Operation

Time Commitment: 10-20 hours/week

Privacy: Public
