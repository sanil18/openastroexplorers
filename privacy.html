4. Your Choices

a. Account Settings: You can update or delete your account information through your account settings.

b. Communication Preferences: You can manage your communication preferences and notifications.

c. Access to Data: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.

d. Deactivation: You can deactivate your account at any time, which will remove your profile information and project contributions.
